Bio

I am a postdoctoral research fellow at the Visual and Interactive Computing Lab, Nanyang Technological University. My broad research interests lie at the intersection of computer graphics, computer vision, and numerical optimization. Specifically, I am interested in problems revolving around shape analysis, deformation, sketch understanding, 2.5D modeling, 3D reconstruction and related areas.
