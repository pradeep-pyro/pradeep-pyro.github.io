Bio

I am a postdoctoral research fellow at the Multimedia and Interactive Computing Lab, Nanyang Technological University. My broad research interests lie at the intersection of computer graphics, computer vision, and numerical optimization. Specifically, I am interested in problems revolving around geometry processing (shape analysis, deformation), sketch understanding, 2.5D modeling, and deep learning (for graphics and 3D vision).
